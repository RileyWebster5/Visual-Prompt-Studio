Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.2 Prompt: Create an artwork that depicts two men on a cliff/rock looking into an abyss of mountains. The medium used will be oil paint. This artwork shows the men on an "adventure" in the forest. The subject matter will be the two men and a small waterfall inside of a canyon, the subject matter style being landscape and figurative. Vocabulary used: Medium: The tool(s) used to create the artwork. Subject matter: The subject of the peice, the main focus point. Subject matter landscape: An artwork or photograph focused on depicting natural scenery, such as mountains, forests, rivers, or fields. Subject matter figurative: The recognizable, real-world imagery—such as people, objects, or landscapes—depicted in a painting, sculpture, or drawing.
